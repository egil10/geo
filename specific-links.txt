Free / public-domain / open-license (best for commercial use, dashboards, reports)
Wikimedia Commons – "Geography of Norway" category (organized by feature type: mountains, fjords, valleys, rivers, glaciers; high-res, mostly CC-BY/CC-BY-SA): https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Geography_of_Norway
Wikimedia Commons – "Landscapes of Norway" gallery (curated by landscape type): https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Landscapes_of_Norway
Unsplash – Norway landscape collection (free, no attribution required, commercial OK, very high resolution): https://unsplash.com/s/photos/norway-landscape
Pixabay – Norwegian fjord images (royalty-free, no attribution): https://pixabay.com/images/search/norwegian%20fjord/
Pexels – Norway fjord photos (free, high-res, commercial use): https://www.pexels.com/search/norway%20fjord/
Rawpixel – fjords collection (filter to "public domain CC0"; also has historical paintings/maps): https://www.rawpixel.com/search/fjords
National Library of Norway (Nasjonalbiblioteket) – digitized maps, historical photographs, picture collections of Norwegian places: https://www.nb.no
Paid / royalty-free stock (largest selection, best for finding a specific named feature)
Adobe Stock – Norway fjord (385,000+ images, strong search by specific place): https://stock.adobe.com/search?k=norway+fjord
Shutterstock – Norway fjord (huge catalogue, HD/4K): https://www.shutterstock.com/search/norway-fjord
Depositphotos – Norway fjords (named lakes/fjords like Lovatnet, Geiranger, Hardangerfjord): https://depositphotos.com/photos/norway-fjords.html
Vista Create (formerly Vista) – Norwegian fjords (royalty-free): https://create.vista.com/photos/norwegian-fjords/
Freepik / Magnific – Norway fjords (free + premium, photos and vectors): https://www.freepik.com/free-photos-vectors/norway-fjords
